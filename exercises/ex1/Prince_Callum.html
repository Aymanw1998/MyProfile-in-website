Prince Callum

"The older half-brother of Crown Prince Ezran of Katolis"

Prince of the kingdom of Katolis and Callum is also a mage, having an affinity with sky magic, and he is the stepson of the King Harrow, the son of the late Queen Sarai and a late unknown man.

Along with Ezran and Rayla, he hopes to return the Dragon Prince, Azymondias, to its mother, the Dragon Queen, to establish peace between the humans and the inhabitants of Xadia.
