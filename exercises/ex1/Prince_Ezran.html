Prince Ezran

"The younger brother of Prince Callum, and the Crown Prince of Katolis"

The Future king of Katolis, and son and heir of Harrow, son of Queen Sarai and the half-brother of Callum.

Along with Callum and Rayla, he hopes to return the Dragon Prince, Azymondias, to its mother, the Dragon Queen, to establish peace between the humans and the inhabitants of Xadia.
